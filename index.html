Designed to defend

CARRISCASES DROP BOXES are made to protect any equipment stored inside by tailoring the FOAM. Foams in different types and density are being used for the ultimate protection of an equipment in due course of any abusive physical condition. Different types of FOAMS used are : XLPE, PU, EPE, PS.
